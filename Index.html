Space Lumber Jacks Concept

In a distant future, a vast, intergalactic forestry corporation explores the cosmos, sending ships to harvest resources from diverse planets, with a focus on lumber. Planetary resources can be sold, used for quests/story, or fuel or food to be consumed by the player.

During interplanetary travels, engaging animations tell the unfolding story.

The player is a worker, one of many clones raised in isolation to work for the forestry company. workers compete with their lives for pay and promotions. Work related injuries and death will be sugarcoated by human resources into cheerful messages broadcast throughout the company.

Your destiny? Conquest, redemption, or apathy. As you rise through the ranks, you make pivotal choices for the company. Guided by biology and chemistry information, and examples of Earth's legacy of innovation and environmental destruction, your decisions mold the narrative.

Gather resources. Gain promotions. Hoard wealth. Destroy the universe.
